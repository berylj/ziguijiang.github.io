Biography

I'm currently an assistant professor in the School of Software Engineering at Sun Yat-Sen University, China. I received the Ph.D degree in computer science and technology from the Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China, in 2019. I have worked as a postdoctoral fellow at Sun Yat-Sen University for years, under the supervision of Prof. Zibin Zheng. My research interests include blockchain, smart contract analysis and service recommendation.

I am the associate editor of International Journal of Systems and Service-Oriented Engineering, and has served as Conference organizer and reviewer of some international conferences, including CollaborateCom 2022, ICSS 2022, BlockSys 2020, BlockSys 2019, IEEE Services 2020, IEEE SCC 2016, ICCSA 2016, CollaborateCom 2016. I also have served as reviewer of some international journals, such as TOSEM, IEEE TSC, ACM TSAS, PPNA, etc.

I am looking for self-motivated students. Please drop me an email if you are interested in.
